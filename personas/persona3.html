Adina is a dedicated veterinarian practicing in Iași, Romania. She is constantly seeking ways to improve her practice and implement new solutions in veterinary medicine, staying up-to-date with the latest advancements and techniques.

Despite her professional dedication, Adina values maintaining a healthy work-life balance. She understands the importance of self-care and strives to find fulfillment from caring for animals for a living.
