Detailed Description

Iuliana Dobrică, born on September 20, 1992, is a fitness trainer, and graduate of the Faculty of Sports and Physical Education, currently residing in Iași, Romania.

She is a joyful person who leads an active lifestyle and really enjoys her job. Iuliana's top priority is health and good nutrition, and that translates across her closest companion, Luna, the golden retriever. They have a very close bond together, and Iuliana is committed to providing Luna with the best possible care and training.

Iuliana's practical goals include teaching Luna new commands and closely monitoring her progress. She is careful about Luna's nutrition and maintains regular contact with veterinarians, in order to stay informed about Luna's health.

In addition to this, Iuliana aspires to strengthen the relationship between herself and Luna. She finds fulfillment in providing Luna with the best care and training. Moreover, she seeks support and encouragement from other dog owners, since Iuliana is a very social person and loves being part of a community.

Iuliana has always faced some issues relating her quest for optimal pet care. She is disappointed by the limited and outdated content and resources available online. Furthermore, she is wary of the abundance of misinformation in the dog care space and seeks only accurate guidance from professionals.

In conclusion, Iuliana needs the optimal tool that caters to all her needs: a tool which is well-organized, customizable, trust-worthy and community-based.
