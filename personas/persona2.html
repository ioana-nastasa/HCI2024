Detailed Description

Vasile is a retired math teacher from Bucharest, who leads a simple, quiet life along with his wife and their senior dog, Bobiță. He has owned Bobiță ever since it was a puppy, and since then, the dog has been the most important thing in Vasile's life.

Vasile wishes to keep Bobiță's mental agility and overall health in the best shape. Despite being a very meticulous man, he is guilty of being a little forgetful and clumsy about some of his dog's needs, such as medication time, or remembering vet appointments.

Vasile wants to focus on simple activities that strengthen his connection with his best friend. He prioritizes quality time together over tiresome training sessions. Above all, Vasile is dedicated to ensuring that Bobiță's final years are filled with love, comfort, and happiness, making each day together special and meaningful.

Finally, Vasile values simplicity and organization. He needs a simple, easy-to-use tool that adapts to his visual impairments issues, since he has been wearing glasses for his poor sight most of his lifetime.
